Penggunaan "To Be"

Pelajari penggunaan am, is, are, was, were, dan will be.

Present Tense (Masa Sekarang)

Digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta, identitas, atau kondisi saat ini.

Pronoun To Be Contoh Kalimat
I am I am a student.
You are You are my friend.
We are We are from Indonesia.
They are They are busy.
He is He is a doctor.
She is She is smart.
It is It is cold today.

Past Tense (Masa Lampau)

Digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta, identitas, atau kondisi di masa lalu.

Pronoun To Be Contoh Kalimat
I / He / She / It was She was sick yesterday.
You / We / They were They were at the party last night.

Future Tense (Masa Depan)

Digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta, identitas, atau kondisi di masa depan. Bentuknya sama untuk semua pronoun.

I / You / We / They / He / She / It will be I will be there tomorrow.
